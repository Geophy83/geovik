About Us

Based in Ashongman Estate, Accra, GeoVik is a premier home and tech solutions firm founded by two brothers. We bridge the gap between high-end physical infrastructure and enterprise-grade digital engineering. Our mission is to transform standard spaces into secure, lag-free digital fortresses. Our Expertise We provide a comprehensive "360-degree" tech ecosystem, focusing on four core pillars: Network Engineering: Designing robust, high-speed Wi-Fi and fiber-optic backbones that eliminate dead zones and latency. Security Architecture: Integrating Ultra-HD 4K surveillance with the professional networking required to power it. Smart Lab Solutions: Deploying high-efficiency Thin Client environments for schools and offices to centralize power and reduce hardware costs. Managed IT Services: Providing end-to-end hardware support, server management, and future-proof tech consulting. The GeoVik Difference We don't just "install gadgets"—we engineer systems. Whether it’s optimizing a home office for maximum productivity or building a smart learning lab from the ground up, we bring the Viking Standard of precision, reliability, and brotherhood to every project.
